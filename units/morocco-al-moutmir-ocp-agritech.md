## Content

Al Moutmir is the corpus's first primary-source-documented Moroccan agritech AI deployment. The program is OCP Group's agritech initiative launched in September 2018 — a multi-service agritech program anchored in OCP's position as the world's largest phosphate / fertilizer producer. The deployment integrates fertilizer-soil-test-based recommendation, sensor-based soil and crop monitoring, and AI-based agronomic decision-support into an Integrated Crop Program (ICP) framework.

**Documented scope (per Al Moutmir corporate + LNT Maroc 2025-2026 bilan + SIAM 2026):**

- **Launched September 2018** by OCP Group
- **OCP Group's position**: Moroccan state-affiliated multinational; world's largest phosphate reserves + world's largest fertilizer producer; structurally significant for AI-fertilizer-recommendation integration
- **Multi-service agritech program**: integrated soil testing, fertilizer recommendation, digital agronomic advisory, farmer training, field-school programme
- **Integrated Crop Program (ICP)** framework: four pillars (per LNT 2025-2026 bilan):
  - **Fertilisation raisonnée basée sur la science du sol** = science-of-soil-based fertilizer-soil-test-based-reasoned fertilization
  - **Soil testing** at scale (OCP-affiliated)
  - **Crop-specific protocol** (cereal, legume, vegetable, arboriculture)
  - **Farmer training and field-school**
- **AI-assisted fertilizer recommendation**: AI models for crop-forecast + soil-test-input → fertilizer recommendation; smart irrigation framework (per aihub.ma 2025)
- **Smart irrigation**: AI + IoT for water management (per aihub.ma 2025)
- **2025-2026 bilan** (per LNT 2025-2026 bilan): Annual programme review including digital platform deployment and platform count
- **SIAM 2026 confirmed deployment** (Salon International de l'Agriculture au Maroc; April 2026 in Meknès): Al Moutmir continues to deploy accessible precision agriculture
