# SmartICE — Inuit Sea-Ice Monitoring + ML

**Operational sea-ice monitoring integrating Inuit Qaujimajatuqangit (IQ) sea-ice terminology with machine learning. Deployed across 9 Inuit communities by 2025.**

## What SmartICE is

SmartICE is a social enterprise that integrates Inuit Qaujimajatuqangit (IQ) sea-ice terminology with ML/edge sensing for community travel safety. **The deployment is operational since before 2020, with Mila-cited deployment growth across nine Inuit communities by 2025** (per Nowosad et al. 2025 *Arctic Science*).

Substantive features:
- Edge sensing for ice thickness + safety monitoring.
- IQ sea-ice terminology integration (Indigenous Knowledge + Western science equals).
- Community travel safety application — operationally distinct from research-only monitoring.
- Social enterprise model — operationally distinct from a research project.

## Framework citation

SmartICE does **not** formally cite OCAP®/CARE/NISR/IEEE 2890-2025 in published documentation, but the deployment operationalises IQ sea-ice terminology alongside ML — the same framework-deployment operationalisation pattern as PolArctic and Salmon Vision.
