## Content

RoboCare is corpus's first primary-source-documented Tunisian agritech AI deployment at scale. Founded in Sfax (Tunisia's second-largest city; Mediterranean-coast startup ecosystem), RoboCare develops an agricultural management platform that integrates **multiple data sources — satellite imagery, drone data, IoT sensors, weather data, and field expertise** — through AI models to enable early detection of crop diseases and stress, optimize resource usage, and improve farm performance.

**Documented scope (per Disrupt Africa June 2026):**

- **Founded in Sfax, Tunisia**
- **AI models** for early detection of crop diseases and stress
- **Multi-source data integration**: satellite imagery + drone data + IoT sensors + weather data + field expertise
- **June 2026**: 216 Capital six-figure investment round (announced 25 June 2026)
- **Expansion target**: African and Middle Eastern markets (per 216 Capital's investment strategy)
- **Substantive disease-and-stress framing**: AI for early-detection before symptoms appear; resource optimization (likely water-related given Tunisian/Maghreb agricultural baseline)
- **216 Capital framing**: "high-potential tech startups capable of delivering concrete answers to the continent's major economic, social, and environmental challenges" (per Hassen Arfaoui, principal at 216 Capital)
