## Content

xFarm Technologies is an Italian tech company, founded 2017 by Matteo Vanotti (a farmer) and co-founders. The platform supports digital farm management, precision agriculture, documentation, sensors, analytics, and supply chain integration.

**Concrete figures from xFarm:**
- 600,000 farms supported.
- 9 million hectares.
- 100+ countries.
- 50+ supply chains.

**Origin story (vendor framing).** xFarm's own account: "In 2017, Matteo Vanotti was looking for a technology solution to manage his family farm. American-style software was not suited to the needs of small European farms, and local providers were unintuitive and very limited." The company was built bottom-up from a single farm's needs, then extended.

**Deployment model.** Smartphone / PC platform, multiple modules, designed for small-to-medium European farms initially. Multi-continent expansion through partnerships (Italy × Argentina for precision agriculture training is named in the press room). Notable: xFarm operates across cooperatives, agronomists, contractors, banks, and input manufacturers — the platform integrates with multiple value-chain actors.

## What this unit is doing in the taxonomy

Anchors the *smallholder × predictive ML / sensors-and-IoT × supply chain efficiency* cell at European scale, complementing the venture-funded Agrosmart unit (LATAM, 100k+ farmers) and the venture-funded Kilimo (LAC water-saving AI).
