## Content

The Prairie grain AI cluster is the **substantive Canadian regional value-chain cluster for grains, protein, and digital agriculture**. Spans three sub-clusters across Alberta, Saskatchewan, and Manitoba.

### Three Prairie sub-clusters

**Sub-cluster 1 — Saskatoon grain AI cluster (Saskatchewan).** Anchored by **Super GeoAI Technology Inc. (Dr. Weiping Zeng)** and **VeriGrain**. The 100-year-old grain grading process is the legacy that the Saskatoon cluster challenges. Super GeoAI Phase 2 launched Android + iOS apps with several hundred farmer downloads; expanded to Canola + peas; integration with VeriGrain. Outcome: 28 → 40 employees. Raven Industries (Regina SK) OMNiPOWER autonomous agriculture; Mojow Autonomous Solutions (White City SK) autonomous farm tools. University of Saskatchewan Crop Development Centre (Pea Genomic Selection Platform).

**Sub-cluster 2 — Winnipeg/Steinbach/Vermilion protein AI cluster (Manitoba + Alberta).** Anchored by **MacDon Industries (Winnipeg MB)** — the **largest single CAAIN-funded project** at $3.84M CAAIN / $9.26M total (Harvesting Automation header AI). **mode40 (Steinbach MB)** carcass cooling AI — ISED Challenge → CAAIN → AAFC Lacombe → 10 commercial plant validation. **Lakeland College (Vermilion AB)** Precision Ranching spans AB + MB + BC.

**Sub-cluster 3 — Calgary/Lethbridge/Olds digital agriculture cluster (Alberta).** Anchored by **Ox + Plow Ag (Calgary AB)** digital marketplace for livestock feed procurement; **SmartGro Bioengineering (Calgary AB)** horticultural lighting AI; **PIP International (Lethbridge AB)** circular AgTech ecosystem; **Metabolomics Innovations + AltaML (Lake Newell Resort AB)** SOIL-HUB data coordination centre; **AUAV Tech (Calgary AB)** A.U.G. Signals DSI partner; **Olds College Smart Farm** EarthDaily × Syngenta invasive threats partner.

### Saskatoon grain AI — substantive details

**Dr. Weiping Zeng** (PhD University of Alberta 2002+, faculty University of Saskatchewan) is a substantive academic entrepreneur: Super GeoAI was founded January 18, 2020. CAAIN funding "served as a lifeline" in late 2021. **Outcome:** 28 full-time employees by late 2024, target 40 by early 2025. Phase 2 launched Android + iOS apps. Expanded to Canola + peas. Integration with VeriGrain.

The VeriGrain sampling technology (Saskatoon SK) is the grain-sampling partner. Together the two Saskatoon companies "bring the grain industry into the 21st century and stop relying on inconsistent, subjective approaches to assessing grain value."

**Raven Industries (Regina SK)** brings **OMNiPOWER** autonomous agriculture — the company's driver-optional agricultural power platform.

**Mojow Autonomous Solutions (White City SK)** — autonomous farm tools, active 2026.

### Winnipeg / Steinbach protein AI — substantive details

**MacDon Industries (Winnipeg MB)** is the world's largest header manufacturer. The CAAIN-funded **Harvesting Automation project** addresses the *highly skilled labour* shortage for combine harvester operators. AI automates harvest header control.

**mode40 (Steinbach MB)** is the AI carcass cooling pioneer. Developed in response to an Innovation Solutions Canada Challenge. After MVP, mode40 moved to **AAFC's Lacombe Research and Development Centre** for further development, then 1-year validation in 10 commercial meat processing plants. The mode40 development path (ISED Challenge → CAAIN → AAFC Lacombe → commercial plants) is the *full Canadian development pathway* for federally-supported AI ventures.

**Lakeland College (Vermilion AB)** uses rumen bolus, GPS ear tags, ultrasound, and **LlamaZOO** (BC-based software company) for digital twin dashboard. Industry partners pay for the technology but recover 40% of costs through CAAIN funding. Lakeland's Agricultural Technology degree programme is the workforce pipeline.

### Calgary / Lethbridge / Olds digital ag — substantive details

**A.U.G. Signals + AUAV Tech** — broadacre drought severity index at 20m regional and 3.5m field-level scales. Sentinel-2/3 + Landsat-8/9 + drones + agroclimatic + soil moisture + CID handheld spectrometers. Four-year window: 2021, 2025, 2026, 2027.

**SmartGro Bioengineering (Calgary AB)** — horticultural lighting AI ($738K CAAIN / $6.41M total). Distinct from Sollum (existing Quebec cycle scan); SmartGro is Alberta-anchored.

**PIP International (Lethbridge AB)** — circular AgTech ecosystem.

**Metabolomics Innovations + AltaML (Lake Newell Resort AB)** — SOIL-HUB data coordination centre.
