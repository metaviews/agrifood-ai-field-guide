## Content

Mondragón Corporation is the corpus's first explicit **cooperative-federation institutional anchor** for *Spanish cooperative AI cluster-pattern*. The federation is the institutional substrate that distinguishes *Spain's cooperative AI cluster* from *cooperative-AI deployments in other cooperative-led economies* — operating since 1956 as a federation of worker cooperatives with revenue at €11.213B in 2024 and 70,000+ workers.

**Documented scope (per Mondragón corporate + Wikipedia 2024 + Guardian 2024):**

- **Founded 1956** as cooperative business project; Basque Country
- **Revenue 2024**: €11.213 billion
- **Workers 2024**: 70,000+ (per Guardian 2024)
- **Cooperative-federation institutional structure**: federation of worker cooperatives; multi-sector scope (industrial / consumer-goods / agricultural / financial / retail)
- **Corporate values encapsulation**: per Mondragón corporate, mission encapsulated in "Corporate Values"
- **Strategic adaptation**: per El País English (2016), Mondragón's cooperative model has historically adapted to changing economic conditions (post-Fagor collapse 2014 + post-pandemic restructure)
- **Multi-language operation**: Spanish + Basque multilingual scope

**Why this matters for the corpus:**

Mondragón is *the* cooperative-cluster-format institutional substrate for Spain. The federation's role for *Spanish cooperative AI* is analogous to the role of *NA-equipment-vendor cluster* for *US AI-deployment-vendor ecosystem*: a single institutional anchor that distinguishes the cluster.

The unit *itself* does not have a substantive AI deployment of record; the *federation* is institutional substrate for *future cooperative-AI deployment analyses* and *current Spanish cooperative AI ecosystem framing*. Worth surfacing as *corpus's first cooperative-federation institutional-anchor unit* — distinct from cooperative-governance-NL JoinData (which is single-tier cooperative-governance-data-rights) and distinct from cooperative-led AI deployment of record (parallel to Mondragón-federation-anchor-tier in cluster-with-three-structures pattern).

**Cooperative-statutory-framework observation:**

Mondragón operates under *cooperative-statutory-framework* in Spain + Basque Country. The cooperative-statutory framework includes:
- One-member-one-vote principle (vs. corporate shareholder-vote)
- Member-surplus-distribution principle
- Cooperative-education-and-formation principle (Mondragón is known for *cooperative university*: Mondragón Unibertsitatea)
- Inter-cooperation principle
- Federation coordination

These cooperative-statutory-framework principles are *structurally distinct* from corporate-statutory-framework principles. The corpus preserves this distinction.
