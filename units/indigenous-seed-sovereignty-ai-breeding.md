### Substantive 2024-2026 anchors

**ITPGRFA Multilateral System** — per FAO Plant Treaty: "The Multilateral System is the International Treaty's truly innovative solution to access and benefit-sharing. It includes 64 of the world's most important [...]" — crop plant genetic resources held in trust under the multilateral system. CGIAR's 700K+ germplasm samples are substantively held in trust under ITPGRFA's Multilateral System (see `units/cgiar-eib-global-south-plant-breeding.md`). The Multilateral System is the substantive global governance frame for plant genetic resources.

**DSI — Digital Sequence Information 2024-2026 negotiations** — substantively the substantive 2024-2026 international-policy frame. Per INFOGM (2024): "In 2024, digital sequence information (DSI) derived from genetic resources was at the heart of international negotiations." Per ABS-Biotrade: "A multilateral mechanism for benefit-sharing from the use of digital sequence information on genetic resources (DSI), including a global fund" is under substantive negotiation.

**Lawson 2024 (cited 25)** *Challenging the existing order of knowledge sharing governance* (Journal of Intellectual Property Law & Practice) — substantive 2024 academic analysis of DSI access-and-benefit-sharing challenges. Substantively frames the substantive tension between multilateral-system governance + bilateral-ABS + DSI digital-data layer.

**Arita et al. 2025 (cited 5)** *Reconceiving Benefit-Sharing and Governance of DSI* (Data Science Journal / CODATA) — substantive 2025 academic framing: "this conference concluded that, although not legally binding, the data control rights on genetic resources belong to the sovereign states." Substantive tension: substantive sovereign-state control vs. substantive multilateral-system governance.

**UN Special Rapporteur on Right to Food Michael Fakhri** — per Down to Earth (2025): "Digital genetics threatens seed sovereignty and farmers' rights." UN Special Rapporteur statements during the ITPGRFA meeting in Lima: substantive UN-level critical voice on DSI's threat to seed sovereignty + farmers' rights. **IPES-Food 2026 *Head in the Cloud*** — substantive 2026 critical-voice primary source. IPES-Food substantively frames digital agriculture + AI-driven breeding as substantive risk to seed sovereignty.

**GRAIN 2023 *Capturing the seed: China's agribusiness expansion at home and abroad*** — substantive 2023 critical-voice primary source on Chinese agribusiness expansion into the global seed market. Substantive AI + gene-editing + GM crops + AI-assisted breeding substantive governance concerns (see `units/longping-yuan-caas-china-seed-ai.md`).

**ETC Group/GRAIN 2025 *Top 10 agribusiness giants*** — substantive 2025 critical-voice primary source on corporate concentration (see `units/bayer-syngenta-corteva-multinational-pipelines.md`).

### Substantive Critical-Voice Themes

**1. Concentration + corporate-IP governance**

Per ETC Group/GRAIN 2025: "Four firms control 56% of global seed market." Substantive concentration observation. Substantive tension: corporate-IP governance vs. multilateral-system vs. farmer-led seed networks. The substantive AI-augmented-breeding pipelines of these four firms substantively extend corporate IP to AI-augmented cultivar IP — substantive 2025-2026 critical-voice concern.

**2. Digital Sequence Information (DSI) — the substantive 2024-2026 governance question**

DSI is the digital/sequence-data layer of plant genetic resources. AI plant-breeding pipelines depend on DSI (genomic sequences, multi-omics data, phenotyping data). The substantive 2024-2026 governance question: who controls DSI? Multilateral-system (per ITPGRFA), bilateral-ABS (per CBD-Nagoya), sovereign-state (per Arita 2025), or open-access?

Per Lawson 2024: the substantive existing order of knowledge-sharing governance is "challenged" by DSI's substantive digital-data nature. Per Fakhri (UN Special Rapporteur): DSI substantively threatens seed sovereignty + farmers' rights.

**3. Farmer-led seed networks + agrobiodiversity**

Per Coomes 2015 (cited 422) + Westengen 2023 (cited 133) + Nabuuma 2022 (cited 76): farmer-led seed networks substantively maintain crop diversity + smallholder farmer livelihoods. AI-augmented breeding pipelines substantively bypass farmer-led seed networks in favor of corporate-corporate + CGIAR + academic-deployment. Substantive 2026 critical-voice concern: AI-augmented breeding pipelines may substantively narrow genetic base + bypass farmer-led seed networks.

**4. Indigenous data sovereignty + CARE Principles**

Per the substantive Indigenous-data-sovereignty framework + CARE Principles (Collective benefit / Authority to control / Responsibility / Ethics): Indigenous communities have substantive authority to control data derived from their lands + knowledge systems. AI plant-breeding pipelines that substantively use Indigenous community seed-knowledge + Indigenous-led seed-bank data should substantively apply CARE Principles. Substantive 2026 deployment gap: most AI plant-breeding pipelines do not substantively apply CARE Principles.

**5. CGIAR + Gates Foundation governance**

Substantive critical-voice observation: CGIAR's substantive funding reliance on Gates Foundation (per `units/cgiar-eib-global-south-plant-breeding.md`) substantively tilts CGIAR breeding priorities toward smallholder-farmer productivity goals over farmer-led seed-sovereignty + biodiversity-conservation goals. Substantive 2026 critical-voice concern.

**6. IPES-Food 2026 substantive digital-agriculture critique**

Per IPES-Food 2026 *Head in the Cloud* — substantive 2026 critical-voice primary source. IPES-Food substantively frames digital agriculture + AI-driven breeding as substantive risk to food sovereignty. Worth naming as the substantive 2026 critical-voice primary source.

**7. PolArctic Sanikiluaq Inuit-led mariculture AI (substantive Canadian-context exemplar)**

Per existing corpus unit (`units/northern-canada-can-ai-2026.md`) — PolArctic Sanikiluaq Inuit-led mariculture AI: "first AI to treat Indigenous Knowledge and Western science as equals, Inuktitut + English." Substantive Canadian-context exemplar of Indigenous-led AI deployment applying CARE Principles.

- DSI multilateral mechanism (substantively under negotiation as of 2024-2026)
