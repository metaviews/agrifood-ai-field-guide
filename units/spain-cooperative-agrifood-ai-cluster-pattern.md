## Content

The corpus's substantive finding on Spanish agri-food cooperatives and AI is **cluster-pattern-with-three-structures** — analogous to the Brazilian seed AI cluster pattern (academic-research-led + multinational-corporate-pipelined + empty Brazilian-origin-corporate-vendor tier). Spanish cooperative AI operates at three distinct tiers:

1. **Peer-reviewed / academic-research-led tier** (García-Lafuente et al. 2026 BFJ; Ciruela-Lorenzo et al. 2020 cited 248; Santos et al. 2024 cited 72; Arévalo-Royo et al. 2025 cited 25; Sadjadi 2023 cited 81)
2. **Mondragón Corporation cooperative-federation institutional-anchor tier** (€11.213B revenue 2024; 70,000+ workers; agricultural / industrial / consumer-goods / financial divisions)
3. **Emerging-single-cooperative AI deployment tier** (COVAP "Reto Innovación Abierta 2025"; Andalusian cooperatives AI discussions; second-degree cooperatives Dcoop framing)

The substantive analytical claim: **Spanish cooperative AI deployment-of-record at primary-source tier is substantially thinner than the academic-literature-discussion tier would suggest**. The corpus's *negative-finding-as-substance* framing here is parallel to the Brazilian seed AI cluster-with-three-structures pattern from the Argentine-beef + Brazilian-seed cycle.

**The IUDESCOOP / UPV CEGEA + HEC Paris authorship pattern** (García-Lafuente IUDESCOOP; Sánchez-Tamarit HEC Paris; Guaita-Martínez UPV CEGEA) signals that *academic-research on cooperative AI* is anchored at:
- Universitat de València (IUDESCOOP = University Institute for Cooperative Research)
- Universitat Politècnica de València (CEGEA = Centre for Business Management Research)
- HEC Paris (Sánchez-Tamarit)

Three-institutional substrate for *academic-research-tier* cooperative AI in Spain.

**Tier-2 — Mondragón Corporation cooperative-federation institutional anchor:**

- **Mondragón Corporation** = federation of worker cooperatives in Basque region; €11.213B revenue (2024); 70,000+ workers
- Substantive consumer-goods / industrial / agricultural / financial divisions
- Mission statement (per Mondragón corporate): "outcome of a cooperative business project launched in 1956"
- Distinct from Spanish agri-food cooperatives specifically, *but* provides the *cooperative-cluster-format institutional substrate* that distinguishes Spain's cooperative ecosystem from the NA-equipment-vendor / corporate-procurement patterns
- Mondragón has been analysed in multiple institutional framings (Guardian 2024; El País English 2016) but specific AI deployment of record at Mondragón agricultural division not surfaced at primary-source tier in this scout
- *Mondragón is the corpus's first explicit cooperative-federation institutional anchor* — distinct from the Dutch JoinData cooperative-governance tier, which is *individual-farm-member-governed*

**Tier-3 — Emerging-single-cooperative AI deployment (substantially below peer-reviewed tier scale):**

- **COVAP** (Cooperativa Agroalimentaria del Valle de los Pedroches; northern Córdoba) — *Reto Innovación Abierta 2025*
  - Open-innovation AI challenge via ANCES and DIHBU ecosystem
  - **Two named AI deployment tracks**: (1) *Ganadería de Precisión con IA* (precision livestock farming with AI for cattle health/feed/welfare); (2) *Nuevas formas de conectar con el consumidor mediante IA* (consumer-engagement via AI personalization + trend analysis)
  - Sectors: dairy, cured Iberian, meat processing with transformation centre, animal feed
  - Renewable-energy model (photovoltaic, biomass, biogas)
  - Located in northern Córdoba; multi-sector Andalusian cooperative
- **Andalusian Cooperativas Agro-alimentarias** (Cooperativas Agro-alimentarias de Andalucía) — AI Day framing
  - "From the field to the algorithm, from the coop to the data, from tradition to intelligence" (June 2025)
  - *Cultural-driver framing* for cooperative AI integration
- **Agromillora olive oil AI integration** — Spanish ag-tech multinational operating olive-oil sector integration; olive sector context = 2.79M hectares, 350,000+ farmers, 13,900 oil mills, €4.215B export value
- **Second-degree cooperatives** like Dcoop (Andalusia olive oil; corpus has been named as "largest agri-food cooperative in Spain") — *AI deployment scope not surfaced at primary-source tier in this scout*

**Negative-finding surface:**
