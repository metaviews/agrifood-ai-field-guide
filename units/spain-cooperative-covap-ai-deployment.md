## Content

COVAP (Cooperativa Agroalimentaria del Valle de los Pedroches) is the corpus's first named **first-degree Spanish agri-food cooperative with AI deployment tracks of record**. The deployment tracks are surfaced via the **Reto Innovación Abierta COVAP 2025** (open-innovation challenge) and the partnership ecosystem spans ANCES Open Innovation, DIHBU (Digital Innovation Hub Industry 4.0), and DIGIS3 project.

**Documented scope (per DIHBU 2025 + ANCES Open Innovation 2025):**

- **Location**: Valle de los Pedroches, northern Córdoba, Andalusia
- **Cooperative governance**: First-degree cooperative (member-facing operations, not a federation)
- **Members**: agricultural and livestock producer-members ("socios")
- **Production sectors**:
  - **Dairy** (lácteo)
  - **Cured Iberian** (ibérico curado) — Iberian-pork-cured products (jamón, paleta, lomo)
  - **Meat processing** (cárnico with transformation centre / centro de transformación)
  - **Animal feed** (alimentación animal)
- **Energy model**: renewable-energy based — photovoltaic, biomass, biogas; "modelo energético sostenible"
- **AI challenge structure** (*Reto Innovación Abierta COVAP 2025*):
  - **Track 1 — Ganadería de Precisión con IA**: precision livestock farming with AI for *intelligent cattle monitoring* for health, nutrition, welfare
  - **Track 2 — Nuevas formas de conectar con el consumidor mediante IA**: new forms of consumer-engagement via AI (personalization of purchase experience + trend analysis)
  - **Track 3 — Optimización energética con biogás y renovables**: energy optimization via biogas + renewables
  - **Track 4 — Reducción de la huella ambiental**: environmental footprint reduction
  - **Track 5 — Mejora de procesos industriales y logísticos** (automatización + análisis predictivo): industrial-process + logistics improvement

**The CorValle — nutrition, animal-welfare-and-data-COVAP framing:** COVAP's framing integrates agronomic, livestock, processing, consumer-engagement and environmental dimensions into a single cooperative AI deployment thesis. This is structurally coherent for a multi-sector first-degree cooperative.
