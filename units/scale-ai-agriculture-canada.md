## Content

**Scale AI** is one of Canada's five **Global Innovation Clusters** (alongside Digital / DIGITAL, Protein Industries Canada, Advanced Manufacturing, and Biomanufacturing). Total federal funding: **up to $284M**. Focus: AI for supply chain resilience. Streams: Industry-led projects + Acceleration + Workforce Development + Ecosystem showcase. Total cumulative Scale AI "collective investment: $750M+" as of July 2026.

The **Scale AI Agriculture & Mining filter** lists **13 named projects** (verified from the Scale AI "Projects — Our Investments" page).

**The cross-cluster pattern is structurally distinctive.** At least 5 named leads (Vivid Machines, Terramera, Agi3, Verge Ag, EarthDaily Analytics) appear in *both* Scale AI and Digital Technology Supercluster. **Same companies, multiple federal funding streams.** This is a substantive observation: Canada's cluster programme architecture allows a single company to stack federal funding across clusters, which materially changes deployment economics.

**The insurance vector (Agi3) is the substantive new anchor.** Agi3 builds agricultural-insurance AI and is deployed with named Canadian equipment dealers (Enns Brothers) and named Canadian farms (Rutherford Farms). **Field-guide gap: zero corpus coverage of agricultural insurance AI as a vector.** C-283 (new).

## Critical context

- **The cross-cluster pattern is real but its implication is uncertain.** Some leads (Vivid Machines, Terramera) genuinely appear in multiple clusters' project portfolios; the structural implication is that federal funding stacks, but the magnitude and conditions are not publicly disclosed.
- **Agi3 is the substantive insurance vector anchor.** Agi3 Ltd. (agricultural insurance AI) is the named entity; specific deployment scale (number of farms, coverage amount, claims processed) is not publicly disclosed.
- **Loblaw × Plotly is a Scale AI project, not the existing Loblaw × Blue Yonder deployment.** Plotly is a data-visualization company; the specific Scope of Plotly's demand-forecast-accuracy AI for Loblaw is not publicly detailed.
- **Nulogy × Mars Canada is the only named Mars × Canadian partnership surfaced.** Whether this is a one-time pilot or an ongoing supply-chain integration is not publicly detailed.
- **Alfred × Fairmont × Auberge Saint-Antoine is industry-framing.** Scale AI's project page is the primary source; deployment scale not publicly disclosed.
