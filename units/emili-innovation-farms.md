## Content

Enterprise Machine Intelligence and Learning Initiative (EMILI), founded 2016 (10 years old in 2026), operates Innovation Farms powered by AgExpert — two Manitoba commercial-scale farm operations where EMILI provides innovators across industry and academia with access to leading-edge equipment, technology, and production practices.

**Concrete figures from EMILI (2026):**
- Two physical sites: Rutherford Farms (5,500+ acres, Grosse Isle) and J.P. Wiebe Ltd. (10,000 acres with ~3,000 acres in potatoes, MacGregor).
- 33 projects in 2026, working with 24 innovators.
- 10,000 acres total operational scale across the two sites.
- "Of the 30+ equipment and technologies being demonstrated and tested on EMILI's Innovation Farms powered by AgExpert in 2026, **a third involve AI**" (EMILI Instagram, 2026).
- Founding member of AIVA Network (May 2026).
- 2026 marks EMILI's 10th anniversary.

**Strategic priorities:**
1. Enable farmer-centric innovation.
2. Advance sustainable technologies and techniques.
3. Increase digital agriculture skills and knowledge.

**Partners (named on EMILI website):** FCC (founding partner), AgExpert (FCC's farm management software; the "powered by AgExpert" branding), BASF, Enns Brothers, Elmer's Manufacturing, John Deere Canada, MacDon, MLT Aikins, OCP North America, Simplot Grower Solutions, ThinkShift, J.P. Wiebe, Rutherford Farms, PrairiesCan, Manitoba Agriculture, Manitoba SCAP. Substantial partner cluster spanning industry, government, and academic.

**2026 AIVA-coordinated projects (from EMILI announcement):**
1. AgExpert Field Sustainability Regional Benchmarking software (FCC's own product)
2. Corteva Agriscience spray drone trials
3. Geco Strategic Weed Management AI tool
4. Miraterra (Gabe AI chatbot)
5. Picketa Systems LENS™ (in-field tissue scanning)

## Why it matters for talks

- 10,000 acres across two commercial farms, 33 projects, 24 innovators in 2026 — concrete scale of Canadian applied research.
- "A third involve AI" quantifies the AI share of EMILI's 2026 portfolio. Useful for talks about Canadian agritech activity.
- EMILI's partner cluster spans industry / government / academic — the industry-association structure can convene across the boundaries that constrain ecosystem development.
- EMILI is the Manitoba-anchored operational testbed for AIVA Network; AIVA could not launch in May 2026 without EMILI's prior 8 years of testbed infrastructure.

## Critical context

- "Powered by AgExpert" branding means FCC's farm management software is the data backbone. Vendor-data-flow question applies.
- John Deere Canada as partner is interesting given the right-to-repair settlement context. Multi-vendor partnerships inside applied research may surface interoperability observations.
- The "farmer-centric innovation" framing is structurally different from vendor-led deployment. EMILI is a non-profit industry-led entity, not a commercial vendor.
- The 30+ tech / 33 projects figure should be interpreted with care — overlap between the two numbers is implied (some tech, some software, some processes).
