# NCIAF — National Circle for Indigenous Agriculture and Food

**Launched 13 June 2024 with FCC support. The only named Indigenous-led agrifood organisation with FCC backing and national scope in Canada. **MOU with Canadian Federation of Agriculture (CFA) signed 2025. **

## What NCIAF is

NCIAF is an **Indigenous-led non-profit** with the mandate to support Indigenous agriculture and food sovereignty and advance reconciliation in agriculture. **It is the operational arm for Indigenous-led agrifood that the framework side has been missing** — FNIGC handles data governance; NCIAF handles Indigenous agrifood practice.

## Substantive institutional details

**Launch:** 13 June 2024 (FCC press release).

**President and CEO:** Kallie Wood.

**Senior Director:** Gabrielle Fallu (formerly Nortera).

**Partnerships:**
- **FCC (Farm Credit Canada)** at launch.
- **MOU with CFA (Canadian Federation of Agriculture)** signed 2025.
- Industry presence: Nortera (Fallu hire is from Nortera); broader ag industry partners.

**Web Hub:** Organised into business / funding / programmes categories — substantive operational support for Indigenous agrifood operators.

## Why NCIAF matters

NCIAF is the institutional anchor the framework-deployment gap analysis has been missing. **The framework side (CARE, IEEE 2890, OCAP, FNDGS) is rich; the operational Indigenous agrifood institutional anchor is NCIAF.** NCIAF is the Canadian operational analog to:

- **Intertribal Agriculture Council (US)** — established 1987, the substantive US Indigenous agrifood operational anchor.
- **Te Mana Raraunga (Aotearoa NZ)** — Māori Data Sovereignty Network; established 2015.
- **ONAMIAP (Peru)** — Organización Nacional de Mujeres Indígenas Andinas y Amazónicas del Perú.

**NCIAF is the youngest of these institutional anchors** (June 2024 vs. US IAC 1987) and is still building operational programmes.
