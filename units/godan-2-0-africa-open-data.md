## Content

**GODAN (Global Open Data for Agriculture and Nutrition)** is the corpus's **most-substantive international open-data coordination body for agriculture and nutrition** — founded **2013 (G8-initiated)** with US + UK leadership. Current framing: **GODAN 2.0** — *"advances a holistic framework for transforming food systems through open data, youth-led innovation, and inclusive digital infrastructure."* The 2016 *Global Data Ecosystem for Agriculture and Food* paper (commissioned by Syngenta with GODAN assistance) articulated the **Five stars of open data** + **FAIR principles** for the agrifood context. Substantive African chapter + 2024 side event on Open Data for Agricultural Innovation.

This unit anchors the **international open-data coordination body** cell of the matrix. Distinct from:
- **CGIAR Open and FAIR Data Assets Policy** — GODAN is international coordination; CGIAR is multilateral agricultural research body
- **Open Data Kit (ODK)** — GODAN is open-data coordination; ODK is mobile data collection framework
- **FarmOS** — GODAN is open-data coordination; FarmOS is farm management application
- **Mozilla State of Open Source AI 2026** — GODAN is agriculture + nutrition focus; Mozilla is general-purpose open-source AI

The substantive distinction: **GODAN is the corpus's most-substantive international open-data coordination body for agriculture + nutrition with substantive 13+ year track record + African chapter + CGIAR partnership + FAIR principles**. The GODAN 2.0 framing — *"advances a holistic framework for transforming food systems through open data, youth-led innovation, and inclusive digital infrastructure"* — is the corpus's most-substantive substantive international open-data coordination framing.

## 1. GODAN's founding and history

### 1.1 The 2013 origin

Per GODAN + Wikipedia + ReliefWeb:

- **GODAN founded 2013** (G8-initiated with US, UK)
- **G8 initiative**: international policy coordination at G8 level
- Per Wikipedia: *"GODAN is an initiative that seeks to support global efforts to make agricultural and nutritionally relevant data available, accessible, and usable for..."*
- Per ReliefWeb: *"The project aims to contribute to increased farmers' yields, improved nutrition for consumers and better evidence-based policy making."*

The 2013 origin is **substantive G8-level international policy coordination**: GODAN was created at the highest level of international policy coordination. Distinct from academic origin (CGIAR) and from corporate origin (Syngenta) and from open-source community origin (Mozilla).

The substantive evolution demonstrates **international policy coordination + corporate partnership + CGIAR partnership + 13+ year track record**.

## 2. GODAN's substantive open-data framework

### 2.1 Five stars of open data

Per 2016 *Global Data Ecosystem for Agriculture and Food* paper:

- **Five stars of open data** — substantive open-data framework for agrifood context
- Articulated in 2016 paper commissioned by Syngenta with GODAN assistance
- Per Open Data Institute / Tim Berners-Lee origin: 5-star open data framework (make your stuff available on the web → make it available as structured data → make it available in a non-proprietary format → use URIs to identify things → link your data to other data)

The Five stars of open data + FAIR principles for agrifood context is the corpus's most-substantive open-data framework for agriculture and nutrition.

### 2.2 FAIR principles for agrifood

Per 2016 paper + per `units/open-data-ecosystem.md`:

- **FAIR principles**: Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Re-usable
- **Adopted by CGIAR + USDA Ag Data Commons + GODAN + national agricultural research systems**
- Per `units/open-data-ecosystem.md`: *"FAIR principles provide a shared standard. Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Re-usable. Adopted by CGIAR, USDA Ag Data Commons, and increasingly by national agricultural research systems globally."*

The GODAN-FAIR alignment is the corpus's most-substantive international coordination of FAIR principles for agrifood.

### 2.3 CGIAR + GODAN partnership

Per `units/open-data-ecosystem.md`:

- **2016 *Global Data Ecosystem for Agriculture and Food* paper**: commissioned by Syngenta with GODAN assistance; substantive CGIAR + GODAN partnership
- **CGIAR Platform for Big Data in Agriculture**: substantive open-data commitment + FAIR Principles + Responsible Data Guidelines
- **CGIAR Open and FAIR Data Assets Policy**: substantive multilateral open-source commitment

The CGIAR × GODAN partnership is the corpus's most-substantive multilateral open-data coordination for agricultural research.
