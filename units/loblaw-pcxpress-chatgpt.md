## Content

On February 12, 2026, Loblaw Companies Limited launched the *PC Express app in ChatGPT* — the first grocery shopping app integrated directly inside OpenAI's ChatGPT surface.

The product uses conversational AI to help Canadians explore menu ideas, curate ingredients, and select products from their local Loblaw store. Loblaw's own framing positions this as the company "solidifying [its] position as a North American leader in artificial intelligence (AI) innovation within the retail sector."

The deployment extends beyond consumer-facing surface. Loblaw is also equipping corporate colleagues with *ChatGPT Enterprise*, and has prior internal deployment of proprietary AI solutions built on OpenAI models — including *Robin* (an AI-powered assistant for store owners and managers) and agentic solutions in supply chain management for inventory accuracy and logistics.
