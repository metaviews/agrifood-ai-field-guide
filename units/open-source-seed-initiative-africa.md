## Content

**Open Source Seed Initiative (OSSI)** is the corpus's **substantive open-source seed-sovereignty movement** — initiated in **2012** by plant breeders + farmers + seed companies + sustainability advocates. OSSI's **"Free the Seed" Pledge** is the corpus's most-substantive open-source licensing methodology for seed-sovereignty: *"to create a means for ensuring that at least some seed and some of the genes cannot be locked away from use by others."* The initiative has substantive **OSSI Seed Company Partners** commercialization + African deployment via **Daniel Wanjama (Kenya Seed Savers Network)** + **Pelum Zambia** + **Swaziland Rural Women's assembly** partnerships.

This unit anchors the **open-source seed-sovereignty movement** cell of the matrix. Distinct from:
- **CGIAR (multilateral agricultural research body)** — OSSI is open-source seed-sovereignty
- **AGRA (Alliance for a Green Revolution in Africa)** — OSSI is open-source seed-sovereignty; AGRA is multilateral deployment-of-record
- **A Growing Culture (open-source seed-sovereignty advocacy)** — OSSI is open-source seed initiative; A Growing Culture is seed-sovereignty advocacy
- **WIPO Treaty on Intellectual Property, Genetic Resources (2024)** — OSSI is open-source seed initiative; WIPO Treaty is international treaty

The substantive distinction: **OSSI is the corpus's most-substantive open-source seed-sovereignty movement with 14+ year track record + African deployment via named actors + IDSov alignment with CARE Principles + WIPO Treaty**. The initiative's framing is substantively **IDSov-aligned** (per `units/indigenous-data-sovereignty.md`) — open-source seed + plant breeder-led + non-restrictive licensing + traditional seed saving + community-mediated.

## 1. OSSI's founding and history

### 1.1 The 2012 origin

Per Bhoomidevi Seeds + Food Tank + ResearchGate:

- **Open Source Seed Initiative (OSSI)** initiated in 2012
- Founded by **plant breeders + farmers + seed companies + sustainability advocates**

- Per ResearchGate: *"The Open Source Seed Initiative was initiated in 2012. Following concerns about the concentration in the seed sector and the rise of..."*

The 2012 origin is **substantive response to seed-sector concentration**: OSSI was created to ensure that some seed + some genes cannot be locked away from use by others. This is the corpus's most-substantive substantive response to seed-sector concentration.

### 1.2 OSSI's "Free the Seed" Pledge

Per Food Tank + OSSI institutional materials:

- **"Free the Seed" OSSI Pledge** — substantive open-source legal commitment
- Per Food Tank (2017): *"We formed the Open Source Seed Initiative (OSSI) to create a means for ensuring that at least some seed and some of the genes cannot be locked away from use by others."*
- **I OSSI-Pledge all my...** — substantive Pledge language for plant breeders + seed companies

The "Free the Seed" Pledge is the corpus's most-substantive open-source seed-sovereignty legal commitment. Distinct from open-source software licensing (MIT, Apache, GPL) — the Pledge is specifically for genetic resources.
