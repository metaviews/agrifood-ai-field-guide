## Quote / Position

> "AI adoption in Canadian agriculture and food is not constrained by technology availability, but mostly by ecosystem factors."

## Context

FCC's load-bearing institutional framing: Canadian AI adoption is constrained by ecosystem factors (connectivity, talent, capital, governance), not by technology availability. The framing is more self-aware than typical institutional AI advocacy and worth engaging with rather than dismissing. Anchors the four constraints / four opportunities framework.
