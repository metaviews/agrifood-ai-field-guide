## Quote / Position

> "This recommended practice supports the appropriate disclosure of Indigenous Peoples' relationships and/or links to all data."

## Context

IEEE 2890-2025 is the world's first global standard for provenance of Indigenous data. Approved September 10, 2025 by the IEEE SA Standards Board; published November 14, 2025 after a 5-year development process (PAR approval June 3, 2020). Operates as a Recommended Practice (industry specification, not binding regulation). Operationalises CARE Principles into industry specifications by detailing the process for describing and recording the *provenance* (origin and history of ownership) of data derived from Indigenous Peoples, lands, and waters. **Critically: explicitly applies to AI/ML industries** — per the IEEE Standards Association, the standard "enhances the integrity of that data, and the likelihood of engaged collaborations with data producers in ways that were previously unthinkable" within "data-generated industries, such as artificial intelligence and machine learning" (Udall Center event description, Feb 18, 2026 webinar).

Worth knowing because IEEE 2890-2025 is the operational implementation layer of the CARE Principles — moving from framework articulation to industry specification.
