## Quote / Position

> "This recommended practice supports the appropriate disclosure of Indigenous Peoples' relationships and/or links to all data."

## Context

IEEE 2890-2025 is the world's first global standard for provenance of Indigenous data. Approved August 2025 after a five-year development process. Operates as a Recommended Practice (industry specification, not binding regulation). Operationalises CARE Principles into industry specifications by detailing the process for describing and recording the *provenance* (origin and history of ownership) of data derived from Indigenous Peoples, lands, and waters.

Worth knowing because IEEE 2890-2025 is the operational implementation layer of the CARE Principles — moving from framework articulation to industry specification.
