## Quote / Position

> "We are an independent data platform that believes in agricultural innovation with heart and soul — but only if you, as a farmer, can make the most of it."

> "Any farmer can pool, control, connect and share data — in a safe, secure and fair way — with agribusiness and innovation partners, and to make sure the data and benefits flow back to the farmer."

## Context

JoinData's mission statements anchor the *operational layer* of the data commons architecture. Distinct from NAPDC (framework layer) and OADA (standards layer). The Dutch cooperative cultural fit is structurally important — JoinData was founded by FrieslandCampina (dairy cooperative), Agrifirm, LTO Nederland (farmers' union), EDI-Circle (accountants), Rabobank (cooperative bank). 16,000+ farmer members; 260 sharing parties; 70 using parties.
