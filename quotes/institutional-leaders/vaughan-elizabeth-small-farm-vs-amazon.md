## Quote / Position

> "Is Amazon going to be our food producer 50 years from now, or Microsoft — or are we still going to have small farmers that provide for their local communities, grow culturally relevant crops, and have resilient community food systems?"

## Context

Vaughan's framing captures the community-food-system lens on data capture — distinct from IPES-Food's corporate-concentration critique and from IDSov's rights-based framing. The community-food-system lens is practitioner-anchored: small farmers providing for local communities, growing culturally relevant crops, maintaining resilient food systems. Worth surfacing in any talk about data governance that engages seriously with small-farm viability.
