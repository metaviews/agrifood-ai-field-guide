## Quote / Position

> "Only 2% of vines damaged with JO, compared to over 10% with tractor drivers."

## Context

Fratantuono's vineyard operation is one of the named Naïo Technologies case studies. The 2%-vs-10%+ damage figure is the most concrete comparison point in the field guide for ground-robot precision spraying. Worth knowing because the *physical damage reduction* is structurally different from the *input reduction* framing of most precision ag claims.
