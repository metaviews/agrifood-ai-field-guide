## Quote / Position

> "I've read the terms and conditions. But giving some of that information to the person that I'm having to buy a three-quarter of a million-dollar machine from just doesn't sit quite right."

## Context

Nelson takes advantage of precision-ag capabilities (feeding spraying data to Bayer Climate FieldView for chemical recommendations; allowing John Deere Operations Center to collect equipment data) but withholds some field-level data because of contract concerns. The Civil Eats article uses his quote to anchor the practitioner-reality critique of proprietary data capture.
