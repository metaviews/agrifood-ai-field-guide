## Quote / Position

> "Canada's AI strategy stops at city limits."

## Context

Neethirajan's pithy framing of Canadian AI policy as urban-centric — the *rural-urban gap* in AI strategy. Worth knowing because the *academic-policy* framing is structurally distinct from the IPES-Food corporate-concentration lens and from Pillaud's cooperative / commons lens. Neethirajan is the Canadian academic-policy critical voice on agritech. His broader framing includes: Green AI (computational sustainability), biological digital twins, cybersecurity as critical infrastructure.
