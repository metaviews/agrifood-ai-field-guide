## Quote / Position

> "Move from AI-for-agriculture to Green-AI-for-agriculture, where computational sustainability is integral to mitigation and adaptation."

> "Food systems are critical infrastructure too. Protecting those systems may prove just as important as protecting banks."

## Context

Two complementary framings from Neethirajan's Frontiers paper (April 2026): Green AI as a research framework (computational sustainability integral to AI deployment) and Cybersecurity as critical infrastructure (food systems as critical infrastructure, with the implication that cybersecurity deserves the same priority as financial-sector protection). Both framings are distinct from the vendor-led productivity-efficiency framing.
