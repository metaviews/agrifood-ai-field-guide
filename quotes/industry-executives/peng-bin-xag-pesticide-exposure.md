## Quote / Position

> "I could smell the acrid stench of pesticide from afar and I thought maybe my unmanned aircraft could help... At least drone spraying would limit the farmers' exposure to the chemicals."

## Context

Peng discovered the potential for drone applications during a 2012 road trip through Xinjiang, where he saw aged farmers spraying pesticides by hand without protection. The origin narrative frames XAG's deployment as a *labour-saving* and *farmer-safety* technology, distinct from the productivity-efficiency framing dominant in US/EU vendor marketing. Also noted by Peng: drones can do the same spraying job in 1/30th the time.
