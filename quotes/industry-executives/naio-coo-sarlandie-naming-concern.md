## Quote / Position

> "Started by farmers for farmers" — founder Matteo Vanotti's framing of xFarm Technologies' origin story (2017).

## Context

Two related paraphrased positions from European vendors:
- **Naïo Technologies COO Sarlandie** (WGCIT 2022 interview): Naïo's positioning as small-farm / specialty-crop / vineyard robotics — distinct from broadacre industrial deployment. Founded by farmers for farmers, with French government / BPI / Mirova / Region Occitanie funding.
- **Matteo Vanotti / xFarm Technologies** (2017): xFarm's origin story — Vanotti was looking for a tech solution to manage his family farm; American-style software was not suited to small European operations.

Both framings reflect the *farmer-origin* positioning common to European agritech vendors — distinct from US vendor positioning (which often emphasizes enterprise scale).
