This scan is the cycle-scan for **Spanish agri-food cooperatives + farming + food + AI**. The substantive analytical claim: *Spanish agri-food cooperative AI operates at cluster-with-three-structures scale with substantially thinner deployment-of-record tier than peer-reviewed academic-discussion tier would suggest — a negative-finding-as-substance pattern parallel to the Brazilian seed AI cluster-with-three-structures from cycle-3.*

**Scope.** Spanish agri-food cooperative ecosystem: Cooperativas Agro-alimentarias de España; Mondragón Corporation; first-degree cooperatives (COVAP; Dcoop; Coren; Central Lechera Asturiana; AN Group); second-degree federation cooperations; Andalusia-region cooperative cluster (olive-oil + Iberian-cured + dairy + meat + animal-feed multi-sector); Mondragón-cluster-format institutional substrate; peer-reviewed academic-research-on-cooperative-AI.

**Cutoff.** 2026-07-19.

**Why this scan now.** The user-requested Spanish cooperatives + farming + food + AI cycle explicitly tests *cooperative-led AI deployment scope* — *the user noted the cycle could end in "no substance"* but provisioned for it. Scout phase found *substantial substance* but at *peer-reviewed + Mondragón-institutional + emerging-single-deployment tiers* rather than at *corporate-vendor-deployment-of-record tier*; deep phase treated the *negative-finding-as-substance* framing as the corpus-valuable finding.

**Method.** Two-phase: scout first (six parallel searches across Spanish cooperative landscape baseline; cooperative-led AI deployment; corporative member-led AI; cooperative-institutional substrate; cooperative AI strategy documents; critical-voice); then deep-cycle drafting (3 units + 1 integrating scan). Each unit gets tier-1 verification where available.

## 2. Deep-cycle drafts — three units drafted

The deep-cycle phase drafted three substantive units + 1 integrating scan:

### 2.1 Spanish cooperative cluster-with-three-structures (cluster-pattern observation)

**Anchor unit:** `units/spain-cooperative-agrifood-ai-cluster-pattern.md`

Spanish cooperative AI operates at *three structural tiers* with substantively different visibility and deployment scale:

1. **Peer-reviewed / academic-research-led tier** — García-Lafuente et al. 2026 BFJ; Ciruela-Lorenzo 2020 cited 248; Santos 2024 cited 72; Arévalo-Royo 2025 cited 25; Sadjadi 2023 cited 81. IUDESCOOP at Universitat de València + UPV CEGEA + HEC Paris three-institutional academic-research-tier substrate.
2. **Mondragón Corporation cooperative-federation institutional-anchor tier** — federation of worker cooperatives; €11.213B 2024; 70,000+ workers; multi-sector scope; cooperative-statutory-framework principles. Distinct from Dutch JoinData (single-tier cooperative-governance-tier only).
3. **Emerging-single-cooperative AI deployment tier** — COVAP Reto Innovación Abierta 2025; Andalusian cooperatives AI Day; Agromillora olive oil; second-degree cooperatives (Dcoop framing).

Substantive analytical claim: *Spanish cooperative AI operates at cluster-with-three-structures scale with peer-reviewed-led Tier-1, Mondragón-institutional-federation-anchor Tier-2, emerging-single-coop deployment Tier-3.*

### 2.2 COVAP — first-degree cooperative cattle-AI deployment

**Anchor unit:** `units/spain-cooperative-covap-ai-deployment.md`

COVAP (Cooperativa Agroalimentaria del Valle de los Pedroches; northern Córdoba; Andalusia) — *corpus's first named Spanish cooperative-led AI deployment-of-record*. Reto Innovación Abierta 2025 challenge via ANCES Open Innovation + DIHBU + DIGIS3 EU-research-project-ecosystem. **5 named deployment tracks**: precision livestock farming with AI for cattle health/feed/welfare (Ganadería de Precisión con IA); consumer-personalization AI; energy optimization; environmental footprint reduction; industrial/logistics process improvement.

Corpus's first Spanish cooperative-led cattle-AI deployment (parallel to Argentine SENASA state-led cattle traceability / Brazilian big-three corporate-led cattle traceability / UAE ADAFSA deployment-of-record cattle substrated but smaller).

**Five-deployment-track-within-single-cooperative observation:** COVAP's 5 deployment tracks within a single first-degree cooperative (dairy + cured-Iberian + meat-processing + animal-feed multi-sector) is corpus's most multi-track single-cooperative deployment scope documented.

### 2.3 Mondragón Corporation cooperative-federation

**Anchor unit:** `units/mondragon-corporation-cooperative-federation.md`

Mondragón Corporation = federation of worker cooperatives; Basque Country; €11.213B revenue 2024; 70,000+ workers; 1956 founding. Substantive multi-sector scope (industrial / consumer-goods / agricultural / financial / retail). Federation operates since 1956 — *the institutional substrate has been built over 70 years; AI integration is a recent overlay on substantially older cooperative-federation substrate*.

Substantive cluster-pattern observation: Mondragón is *federation-institutional Tier-2 substrate* — distinct from Brazilian seed AI Tier-2 *multinational-corporate-pipelined* substrate. *Parallel cluster-with-three-structures pattern with structurally-distinct second tier.*

### 2.4 Substantive negative-finding observation (the corpus-valuable outcome)

The substantive corpus-valuable observation from this cycle is *the deployment-of-record tier is substantially thinner than the peer-reviewed academic-discussion tier would suggest*. This is parallel to the Brazilian seed AI cluster-with-three-structures pattern from cycle-3 (which observed *Brazilian seed AI cluster is academic-research-led + multinational-corporate-pipelined + substantially empty at Brazilian-origin-corporate-vendor tier*).

The Spanish cooperative AI pattern observes: *Spanish cooperative AI cluster is peer-research-led + Mondragón-federation-anchor + emerging-single-coop deployment*, with deployment-of-record substantially below academic-discussion-tier scale.

The parallel-pattern observation (Spanish cooperative AI vs. Brazilian seed AI) is corpus-substantive — both surfaces have *academic-discussion-tier > deployment-of-record-tier*. The structurally-distinct second-tier observation is corpus-substantive (Spanish cooperative = federation-institutional-substrate; Brazilian seed = multinational-corporate-pipeline-substrate).
